About Me

Hi! My name is Maria Hannah Mae Zuela Paulino, a 2nd year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student at STI Colleg Ortigas-Cainta. My career journey stemmed from the curiousity that grew in me upon seeing my father being into various types of consoles and video games. Having a close relationship with my father growing up, I hve witnessed how much he likes to venture paths that are related to technology. Hence, I, myself, have also gained interest in this field.


Though I already have the interest in the industry, I did not have enough courage to venture such path because deciding for a journey to go into is scarier than everyone might think. I believe that my turning point was when my sister took Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. I have seen her do what I regard back then as "cool stuffs" with only just her laptop. And so, I decided to be also cool myself.


In 5 years, I see myself as a leading expert in software development, creating innovative applications that enhance user experiences and improve everyday life. Though coding might be complex at times and involves being in a challenging environment, I can continuously learn and apply my existing knowledge to develop solutions that make a real difference.


Growing up in a very loving and understanding family, I recognized the value of every opportunity and the sacrifices made for anything at an early stage. This perspective fuels my drive to excel and motivates me to come after every opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully to society. Moreover, my dedication to my goals and personal values, together with my deep-rooted passion for technology and commitment to social responsibility, distinguishes me as a person who is not only capable but also deeply invested in making a difference which is what matters the most in this fast-evolving world.
